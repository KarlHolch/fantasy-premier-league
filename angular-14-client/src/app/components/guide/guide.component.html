This is more than just a game; it's a season-long quest for glory, where every point can make a difference. Are you ready to compete for the top prizes? The leaderboard awaits, and the top three managers will be rewarded handsomely for their strategic brilliance.

Embrace the challenge, outmaneuver your rivals, and lead your team to victory. The Classic League is calling—will you answer?

Sprints

Every five gameweeks, the competition intensifies as we introduce a new sprint with its own distinctive set of rules, creating a fresh challenge and a chance to climb the leaderboard.

Stay alert for the announcements and be ready to adapt to the new rules. It's not just about the long game; it's about being quick, agile, and responsive to the ever-changing dynamics of the league.

Each sprint is a mini-tournament within the larger fantasy league, operating on a separate point system. Imagine a sprint where the usual suspects for points are turned on their head—where only yellow cards yield points. It's a test of your predictive skills and your ability to think outside the box.
